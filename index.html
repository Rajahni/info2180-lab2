The iPhone 14 Pro

September 29, 2022 by Rajahni Cunningham


On September 7, 2022, Apple revealed the brand new Iphone 14 Pro and Pro Max. These phones are very similar to their predecessors, however, they come with a brand new A16 processor, a new camera system and engine that Apple calls "Photonic Engine". The newest, most distinguishable feature, is the brand new "Dynamic Island."

Dynamic Island is indeed an eye catching work of art. The newly designed notched which is now a pill shaped cut-out has been designed to highlight status notifications such music playing, timer set, FaceID unlock etc. Smoothly animated tabs and widgets expand from the cut-out as is needed, giving users ease of access to view live states of applications. Apple has undoubtedly created a new feature that will enhance the user experience of their device.
